Última actualización: 18 de mayo de 2026

La aplicación Conversor de Unidades (en adelante, «la App») respeta su privacidad. Este documento explica qué información se maneja y cómo.

Recolección de datos

La App no recolecta, almacena, transmite ni comparte ningún dato personal. No se requiere registro, cuenta de usuario, ni conexión a Internet para usar la App.

Almacenamiento local

Para mejorar su experiencia, la App guarda únicamente en su dispositivo las siguientes preferencias:

  • Modo seleccionado (Básico / Avanzado).
  • Especialidad y categoría activas.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

The Conversor de Unidades application (the "App") respects your privacy. This document explains what information is handled and how.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. No registration, user account, or Internet connection is required to use the App.

Local Storage

To improve your experience, the App stores only on your device the following preferences:

  • Selected mode (Basic / Advanced).
  • Active specialty and category.
  • Preferred source and destination units per category.
  • Result decimal places.
  • List of conversions marked as favorites.

This data never leaves your device and is removed when the App is uninstalled.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use analytics, advertising, third-party SDKs, or cloud services. It works 100% offline.

Children

The App is suitable for all ages. Since it collects no information of any kind, it is safe for minors.

Changes to this Policy

If this policy is modified in the future, the new version will be published at this same URL with the corresponding update date.
